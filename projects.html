Projects

These projects illustrate the breadth of my work, from immunological assays and long COVID classification to community health and immigrant rights.

Viral Shedding in Post‑Vaccination Infection

This project investigates how baseline neutralizing antibody levels from vaccination affect viral load and the duration of infectious virus shedding. Higher antibodies were linked to lower viral loads and shorter shedding periods in Delta infections.

Multiplex Serology Platform

By using mass cytometry and isotopically barcoded beads, this initiative develops a high‑throughput serology platform that measures antibodies against multiple antigens in parallel, reducing cost and time.

Long COVID Research Index

Our team is refining a classification system for long COVID to reflect the condition’s complexity and incorporate new data as it becomes available.

Early PASC Biomarkers

We collect repeated nasal specimens during acute infection to understand viral dynamics and immune responses that may predict post‑acute sequelae.

Park Rx Community Collaborative

This project evaluates the Santa Clara County Park Rx program that prescribes nature walks for children and caregivers. Surveys and focus groups show increased social connectedness, appreciation of nature and reduced stress among Latino families.

American Dreaming – Resilience for Undocumented Storytellers

This collaboration documents stories of 40 undocumented or formerly undocumented participants. Using composite narratives and mixed methods, it highlights mental health, resilience, agency, support, inclusion, legacy and solidarity within the immigrant rights movement.

Health of Immigrants Released from Detention

In partnership with clinicians, this study explores the physical and mental health status of immigrants after their release from detention, identifying unmet healthcare needs and advocating for better support.
