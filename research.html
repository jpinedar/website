Research

My work explores how viral dynamics, immunity, public health and community resilience shape disease outcomes. I use clinical, laboratory and community‑based studies to understand how the body responds to infection, vaccination and broader social determinants of health.

Key areas of focus include:

  • How neutralizing antibodies influence viral load and infectious virus shedding after vaccination.
  • Development of high‑throughput serology methods to measure antibodies against multiple antigens.
  • Refining classification systems for long COVID and studying early biomarkers of post‑acute sequelae.
  • Evaluating the mental health and resilience of undocumented storytellers and immigrant families, using composite narratives and mixed methods.
  • Studying the impact of Park Rx programs on Latino families’ social connectedness and stress reduction.
  • Assessing health and wellbeing of immigrants released from detention and advocating for improved care.
  • Monitoring viral RNA and culturable virus in tears and in wastewater to inform public health surveillance.
